The Problem

Modern vehicles collect and transmit large amounts of driver, location, and behavior data, but owners have no simple way to understand what is being gathered, what can be disabled, or how to document privacy settings for compliance or resale.

Our Solution

DrivePrivacy is a consumer privacy assistant that scans a vehicle make/model, explains its data collection footprint in plain English, gives a step-by-step disable checklist, and generates a shareable privacy report for owners, fleets, and auto brokers.

Key Features

  • Vehicle-specific privacy profiles by make, model, and year
  • Guided disable/opt-out walkthroughs for connected car settings
  • Downloadable privacy audit report with risk score
  • Change tracking for firmware or policy updates
  • Fleet dashboard for monitoring privacy posture across vehicles
